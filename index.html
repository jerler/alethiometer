    How to Read

    The alethiometer is a mystical instrument that reveals truths, but only to those who know how to read it. Its answers are given through the layered meanings of its 36 symbols. Concentrate on your question and let the Dust of the universe flow through the compass.

    Inspired by the truth-telling device from His Dark Materials by Philip Pullman, this digital version invites you to practice your own reading, and uncover hidden truths in your life.

    To make a reading

    First, ask your question by setting the three controllable arms to relevant symbols. Use the dials, drag the arms, or set them directly from the Arms tab. When ready, concentrate on your question by clicking "concentrate" or by double-clicking the center of the alethiometer.

    The fourth arm then gives the answer. The more times it lands on a symbol, the deeper the meaning it's trying to convey. Ex: Hourglass may mean "time" when pointed at once, but with repeated turns it might really suggest "impermanence", "legacy", or even just "sand". The reading is yours to interpret.

    To guide you, we have included a dictionary of some possible meanings for each symbol, but every reader must learn to communicate through their own interpretations.
